Cancellation Options

We understand plans change. BookItNow is committed to clarity regarding cancellations.

  • Policy Varies: Every property sets its own cancellation policy, which is clearly displayed before you book and in your confirmation email.
  • Free Cancellation: Many listings offer a "Free Cancellation" window, allowing you to cancel without charge up to a specified date/time.
  • Manage Online: You can initiate cancellations or modifications directly through the My Bookings section of your account. Review refund conditions on your booking page before confirming changes.

If you need assistance with a cancellation, please contact our support team via the Contact Us page.
